Unlocking the power of digital billboard advertising with DigiAds

Digital billboards are revolutionizing outdoor advertising, offering dynamic content that captures attention in high-traffic areas. With the rise of digital marketing, traditional media may seem outdated, but digital billboards provide a fresh avenue to reach audiences weary of digital clutter.

What is a digital billboard and how it works?

A digital billboard, also known as a programmable billboard or jumbotron, utilizes LED technology to display dynamic content remotely. Typically found in high-traffic areas like highways, malls, or stadiums, they offer businesses the flexibility to quickly change ad content, targeting specific audiences or timeframes.

How do digital billboards fit?

Oftentimes, digital billboards fit into marketing strategies as part of the branding efforts. They’re not really the channel that you would do performance marketing on since you don’t have a “shop” button readily available to click. As such, big brands would use them to create awareness and engage with the audience in hopes of getting them brand-hooked in order to make a purchase. Of course, you can also display sales-ready messages like offers “buy one, get one free” but that is something that you will most likely see on the static ones, where the visuals don’t actually change. But all this stuff is just for the big brands. How can any SME or startup hope to leverage that kind of power on a short budget?
